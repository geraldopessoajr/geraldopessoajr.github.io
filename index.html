On Interdisciplinary Collaborations in Scentific Coauthorship Networks




Project developed by the master student of the DCC/UFMG, Geraldo Jos√© Pessoa Junior, under the supervised of Prof. Dr. Alberto Henrique Frade Laender. This project aims to analyze the impact of the interdisciplinarity of collaborations in scientific coauthorship networks. The Lattes Platform was used as a database for the generation of Interdiciplinary and Global coauthorship networks, and some results are presented here. The percentage of collaboration among the eight major areas proposed by Lattes (Agrarian Science, Applied and Social Science, Biological Science, Engineering, Exact and Earth Science, Health Science, Humanities and Linguistic, Letters and Arts), the analysis considering the five geographic regions of Brazil (Center-West, North, Northeast, South and Southeast),the analysis the academic age of the researcher considering three time intervals (up to 10 years, 10-20 years, and over 20 years) and finally, the temporal analysis of the quantity of publications between two great areas.
